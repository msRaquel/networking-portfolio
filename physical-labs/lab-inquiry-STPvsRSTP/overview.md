## Reasearch Inquiry
Based off research done by Parita Rathod (Electronics & Communication Department RK University, Rajkot,Gujarat, India) compared down time when switches use the Spanning Tree Protocol (STP) vs. the Rapid Spanning Tree Protocol. 

The purpose of this analysis is to confirm numerical differences between RSTP finding a new path versus STP. The difference between this lab and the research it is based off, is the access to physical switches. The switches used in lab are Cisco Catalyst 3750 switches. 

An acitvity created by Ivan Mizuik (ivan info here) was provided to simulate the research.
